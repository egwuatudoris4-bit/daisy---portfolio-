Hook Library

10 proven wig hooks
engineered to stop scrolls.

```
01
POV Contrast
POV: You spent $300 at the salon and it still doesn't look like THIS 👀
Why it works: Creates immediate contrast between pain (wasted money) and solution. Stops the scroll with relatability.
02
Curiosity Gap
I've been keeping this wig secret for 6 months… and today I'm finally showing you why 👇
Why it works: Creates urgency + mystery. Viewer must watch to close the information gap.
03
Bold Claim
This $89 wig made three people stop me at Target to ask who did my hair 😭
Why it works: Social proof baked into the hook. Real-world validation creates desire instantly.
04
Transformation
Watch me go from stressed edges to snatched in literally 15 minutes flat ⏱️
Why it works: Before/after transformation hooks get 3x more watch time. The timer creates urgency.
05
Controversy / Hot Take
I'm sorry but cheap wigs actually slap harder than expensive ones — here's proof 🧾
Why it works: Challenges a common belief. People comment to agree or disagree = algorithm fuel.
06
Identity / Belonging
If you think lace wigs are too complicated — this video is literally for you 🫵
Why it works: Speaks directly to an identity. Makes the viewer feel personally called in, not out.
07
Story Setup
So my sister called me crying because her wig looked a mess for her date… so I fixed it in 10 minutes 😅
Why it works: Narrative tension + emotional stakes = viewer watches for the resolution.
08
Number / List
5 signs your wig is aging you (and exactly how to fix each one) ✋
Why it works: List format signals quick value. "Aging you" triggers vanity — one of the strongest emotional drivers.
09
Pattern Interrupt
Wait— you're installing your wig WRONG and it's ruining the lace 😬
Why it works: Accusatory but helpful. The "wait—" forces a pattern break mid-scroll. Fear of doing it wrong drives clicks.
10
Dream Outcome
Imagine waking up, throwing on a full glam wig in 8 minutes, and walking out the door looking like this 💅
Why it works: Paints the dream outcome vividly. Taps into the desire for ease + beauty simultaneously.
```
